Fresh Track

UI/UX Design (Bilkent University Exchange)

Overview

Conceived during my exchange program at Bilkent University, Fresh Track is a sustainability-driven digital platform designed to close the "Purchase-to-Consumption" gap and reduce household food waste in student dormitories.

Technical Execution

Overcoming User Friction: Conducted behavioral research identifying "Data Entry Fatigue" as the primary barrier to inventory tracking. To solve this, I designed an automated "Smart Scan" flow.

AI & Ecosystem Integration: Leveraged AI extraction concepts to pull itemized grocery data and estimated expiry dates directly from receipts, optimizing the platform specifically for seamless synchronization with the Migros app ecosystem.

High-Fidelity Prototyping: Built comprehensive Figma prototypes featuring a color-coded inventory dashboard to visualize shelf-life.

Circular Economy Design: Engineered a "Hub Mode" feature, allowing students to securely list and share surplus food items within their housing complex before expiration.
