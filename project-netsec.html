Corporate Network Vulnerability Assessment

Cybersecurity & Information Assurance

Overview

A comprehensive cybersecurity assessment evaluating the resilience of a simulated corporate network against modern cyber threats, utilizing a proactive "Red Team" methodology.

Technical Execution

Active Exploitation: Conducted deep network reconnaissance and actively exploited critical vulnerabilities, specifically CVE-2017-0144 (MS17-010), to practically demonstrate the mechanics of full-scale data exfiltration on unpatched systems.

Industry-Standard Compliance: Modeled the risk analysis and reporting framework against Maybank’s public security guidelines to ensure the assessment met the strict regulatory and compliance requirements of the Malaysian financial sector.

Strategic Remediation: Delivered a comprehensive roadmap advocating for a "Defense-in-Depth" strategy. This included the technical rollout of Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA), enforcing the Principle of Least Privilege, and deploying a centralized SIEM system for real-time threat monitoring.
